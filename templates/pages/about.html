About DaryMeal

DaryMeal is an app put in place where any individual near a certain residential area can be able to identify a shop, hotel and/or orphanage that is registered with us and make a payment however small thus contributing to feed any starving individuals around you as charity begins at home. The transactions will be regulated on a block chain platform to allow for credibility and openness. The meals/snacks will be prepared and packaged then delivered to any affected areas and individuals by the caterers.
